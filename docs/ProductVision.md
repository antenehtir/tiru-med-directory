# DigitalDirectory-v2

## Mission

Become Ethiopia's most trusted healthcare discovery platform.

---

## Primary Users

### Patients
People searching for:
- Doctors
- Hospitals
- Clinics
- Pharmacies
- Diagnostic centers
- Healthcare information

### Doctors
Healthcare professionals who want:
- Visibility
- Verification
- Patient discovery
- Professional presence

---

## Core Principles

### Search First
Users should find healthcare quickly.

### Trust First
Verified healthcare facilities and professionals.

### Mobile First
Designed primarily for Android smartphone users.

### Simplicity
Reduce confusion and decision fatigue.

---

## Version 2 Core Features

### Healthcare Search
Search by:
- Facility
- Specialty
- Doctor
- Service

### Nearby Healthcare
Find healthcare facilities near the user.

### Verified Facilities
Display trusted verification badges.

### Verified Doctors
Professional verification system.

### Specialty Discovery
Easy access to medical specialties.

### Pharmacy Discovery
Locate pharmacies quickly.
